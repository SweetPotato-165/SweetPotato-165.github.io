Researching financial harm in young people and how we can prevent it.

I'm Katie Thomas. I research how families communicate about money, what financial harms young people face today, and how education and support can help. My work is research-led and people-centred with real-world outcomes at its core.

Featured work

Workshops

I've delivered challenge-based workshops in collaboration with Bath College, using these sessions to bring awareness of online financial harms among young people. These workshops provide an engaging, hands on education on financial harms while promoting creative problem-solving and presentation skills in students.

Investigating the balance of privacy versus security in online family banking

Digital banking for children has become much more common, but little research has looked at how it affects family money management or whether these services meet families' needs. The findings intend to help identify how families use these services and highlight ways they could be improved.
